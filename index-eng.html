QUALITY ASSURANCE SYSTEM IN UKRAINE: DEVELOPMENT ON THE BASE OF ENQA STANDARDS AND GUIDELINES.

The project QUAERE «Quality Assurance System in Ukraine: Development on the Base of ENQA Standards and Guidelines» is one of the ten projects supported by the European Commission in Ukraine and the only one with the national level of results’ implementation. It belongs to Key Action 2 – Capacity building in the Field of Higher Education 2015 (ex-TEMPUS).

The project «Quality Assurance System in Ukraine: Development on the Base of ENQA Standards and Guidelines» was developed by the staff of Sumy State University in close cooperation with Polish and German experts. The project’s coordinator is Wroclaw University of Science and Technology. It has a budget of € 717 thousands. The QUAERE project aims to improve the sustainable functioning of internal and external quality assurance in higher education, ensure capacity building of universities and national level institutions through staff professional development, building organizational infrastructure quality assurance in higher education, and introduce the introspection mechanism as an integral component of universities’ functioning. Duration of project is two years.

The specific objective targets

Promoting a better understanding of HEIs and EQAs of the role of NQFs and SQFs, their structure as well as the differences between the different KINDS and LEVELS of student achievement
Building on the capacity of HEIs to write and assess learning outcomes that define the various types of student achievement and promote student-centered teaching and learning
Building on the capacity of the HEIs to use the QF alignment to facilitate student transfer, joint qualifications and benchmarking
Enabling the EQAs to check whether proposed learning outcomes and their assessment mechanisms match the QF descriptors at each level by establishing mechanisms for ensuring consistency of judgments across institutions
